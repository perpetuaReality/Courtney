About

Courtney

is a little Discord bot that let's you keep track of your character's healthbars. For example, for when you play D&D or any other RPG systems with your friends!

Courtney let's you keep a backlog of characters whose HP levels you can modify on the fly and check at any time. She also allows you to visualize these with neat little text-based healthbars.

That's pretty much it, really!

Kind of a one-trick pony, but she does her job perfectly!

And if she doesn't, please, contact me!

Although, well, you can also interact somewhat with her with more than just commands! You can also say hello to her, wish her a goodnight, or tell her you love and appreciate her, if you so want!
